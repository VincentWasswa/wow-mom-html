FAQs

Where are you located?
I currently live in San Jose, CA and am happy to support births in the South Bay, Peninsula, and East Bay Areas. For clients outside my driving distance, virtual support packages are available.
Do you support home births?
Absolutely. I believe everyone should be able to labor and birth where they feel most comfortable to enable a smooth and blissful experience. If your midwife deems a transfer is necessary, I will support you in that time of transition. If you are seeking a freebirth experience, I can offer virtual support only.
What is neurodivergent and sensory support?
A hospital labor ward can be a stressful environment for any laboring person, and even moreso for people with neuro processing differences. Drawing from my lived experience as an autistic woman in labor and your personal reflections on how sight, sound, touch, smell, and taste can affect your mood, we will work together to plan how to reduce interruptions to your natural hormone flow and maximize your comfort during labor.
What if I want an epidural or other medical pain relief?
No judgement here! While I am an avid believer in the female body’s ability to give birth naturally, everyone’s perception and experience is different. During our first prenatal visit we will discuss your desires surrounding pain relief and inform you of the options that may be available to you.
